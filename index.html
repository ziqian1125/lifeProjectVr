We bring virtual reality to the Political Science course and vocational courses

The aim of the project

1.Different university lecturers(political science and vocational) use Virtual Reality (VR) in their classes

2.Students experimenting, learning by gaming and virtual interaction

Interdisciplinarity approach

To design the teaching plan for the political science and vocational training,we use approach like interview and user testing. In the interview,we get more information from our teacher:the targeted user,the demands,knowledge about the subjucts etc. In the user testing,we let teachers try the vr game we choose.By actually trying these games,we get feedback what kind of game are more suitable for the classroom and targted users.
